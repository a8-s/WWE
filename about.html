About

There are few things in life that we will always need and water is one of them. The earth is growing warmer
and we are wasting water at astronomical levels. We are trying to make these issues known. When a source of
water is contaminated and polluted, do we ignore it? Or do we tell the world. That is what we are trying to do.
Please, fill out our form if you come across a polluted water source or even know about something affecting the water.
